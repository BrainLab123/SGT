Global Expertise and Quality Power

Modern businesses are regularly disrupted by electric power quality events: lightning strikes, harmonics, voltage sags and swells, brief interruptions, and dozens of other types of disturbances. At the same time, high-tech sources of energy have begun to displace the stability provided by traditional massive rotating generators. And today's commercial and industrial operations rely increasingly on power-sensitive devices. That's why, everywhere in the world, power quality problems are getting worse. Our customers will benefit from a unified set of monitoring, diagnosing, trending, and mitigation capabilities that solve power quality disturbances.
